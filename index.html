Technical Indicator Explanation

1. RSI Bullish Divergence (50 Points)

The primary entry signal. This occurs when the price makes a new low but the RSI makes a higher low, signaling weakening downward momentum and a likely reversal.

2. MACD Confirmation (20 Points)

A strong confirmation signal. Points are awarded for a bullish MACD crossover (fast line crossing above slow line) that occurs below the zero line, indicating a potential reversal from a downtrend.

3. Overall Trend Confirmation (20 Points)

Confirms the underlying trend is favorable. Points are awarded if the price is trading above the 200-period EMA (10 pts) and the 55-period EMA (10 pts).

4. Supporting Factors (10 Points)

Points are awarded for higher than average volume (5 pts) and high volatility (5 pts) to confirm interest and opportunity in the coin.
