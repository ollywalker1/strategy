Scoring System

The "Scan Score" is calculated based on the following criteria for your selected timeframe. The "Confluence Score" is a weighted average of scores across multiple timeframes for the top-ranked coins, giving more importance to your primary scan timeframe.

RSI (50 Points Total)

  • ✓ RSI Bullish Divergence: +50 pts
  • (Visual Only) RSI in Bull Zone (RSI > 40)

MACD (20 Points Total)

  • ✓ Bullish Crossover from Negative: +20 pts
  • (Visual Only) Histogram Ticking Up

Trend (20 Points Total)

  • ✓ Price > 200 EMA: +10 pts
  • ✓ Price > 55 EMA: +10 pts
  • (Visual Only) Golden Cross

Other Factors (10 Points Total)

  • ✓ High Volume: +5 pts
  • ✓ High Volatility: +5 pts
