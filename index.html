Dual Golden Cross Strategy Explanation

Strategy Overview

This strategy identifies bullish entry signals based on two types of "Golden Cross" events, where a shorter-term Exponential Moving Average (EMA) crosses above a longer-term one. Each potential entry is validated by additional trend, momentum, and volume indicators.

Entry Option 1: 21/55 EMA Golden Cross (Medium-Term Signal)

  • Primary Signal: 21 EMA crosses above 55 EMA.
  • Trend Confirmation: Both 21 & 55 EMAs are above the 200 EMA.
  • RSI Filter: RSI (14) is above 50.
  • Volume Confirmation: Volume is at least 1.2x the recent average.
  • Price Action: Price closes above both the 21 and 55 EMAs.

Entry Option 2: 55/200 EMA Golden Cross (Long-Term Signal)

  • Primary Signal: 55 EMA crosses above 200 EMA.
  • Momentum Confirmation: 21 EMA is already above the 55 EMA.
  • RSI Filter: RSI (14) is above 50.
  • Volume Confirmation: Volume is at least 1.5x the recent average (stronger signal).
  • Price Action: Price closes above all three EMAs (21, 55, 200).
